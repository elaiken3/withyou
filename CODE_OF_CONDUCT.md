# Code of Conduct

WithYou is committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment.

Harassment, discrimination, or dismissive behavior — especially toward
neurodivergent experiences — will not be tolerated.

---

## Our Standards

We expect contributors to:
- Be kind and patient
- Assume good intent
- Respect lived experiences
- Avoid dismissive or ableist language

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or insults
- Dismissing ADHD or neurodivergence
- Shaming productivity struggles
- Hostile or aggressive communication

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or contributors who violate this code.

This code exists to protect psychological safety — not to restrict discussion.
